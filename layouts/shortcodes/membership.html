Key Services of the Eclipse Foundation

The OpenHW Group works closely with the Eclipse Foundation to deliver these key services to our members:

Development Process based on Industry Best Practice

Our staff guide OpenHW members in the best-practice Eclipse Development Process, already proven in many open-source Eclipse Foundation projects, and applied to open-source hardware development by OpenHW Group.

IP Management

We use the Apache 2.0, Solderpad 2.0, and the Eclipse Public License (EPL) as proven frameworks for open source hardware and software licensing. Our IP team applies world class expertise in IP provenance validation, vital for open-source projects.

Ecosystem Development and Marketing

Our Marketing Working Group works to bring CORE-V technologies and developments to the fore through conferences, OpenHW TV, and marketing events with members.

IT Infrastructure

We leverage the vendor-neutral, reliable and scalable technology infrastructure of the Eclipse Foundation to deliver source code repositories, CI infrastructure, mailing lists, messaging platforms, and other tools. These enable a seamless, effective open-source experience for our members.

Membership at OpenHW Group

OpenHW Group offers four levels of membership: Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Supporter.

Platinum Member

Platinum members view participation in OpenHW Group and the development of a global ecosystem of open-source, industrial-quality processor IP as strategic to their business, and they play a leading role in all aspects of OpenHW Group. They invest significant developer and other resources to advance OpenHW technology, capabilities and scope, while leading technology projects and technical/industry committees. Platinum Member benefits:

  • An employee is eligible for board-seat elections.
  • Employees are eligible to serve as Chair or Vice Chair of committees.
  • Entitled to cast one vote on any matter presented to the Members (including the election of directors).
  • Employees are entitled to join and make technical contributions to all OpenHW Group technical committees and projects.
  • Entitled through their OpenHW Group and Eclipse Foundation memberships to join the OpenHW Asia and OpenHW Europe Working Groups.
  • Receive a complementary Eclipse Foundation Contributing Membership.

Gold Member

Gold members play a prominent and leading role in the development of OpenHW Group's global ecosystem of open-source, industrial-quality processor IP. They invest significant developer resources while leading OpenHW technology projects and chairing technical committees. Gold Member benefits:

  • Employees are eligible to serve as Chair or Vice Chair of committees.
  • Entitled to cast one vote on any matter presented to the Members (including the election of directors).
  • Employees are entitled to join and make technical contributions to all OpenHW Group technical committees and projects.
  • Entitled through their OpenHW Group and Eclipse Foundation memberships to join the OpenHW Asia and OpenHW Europe Working Groups.
  • Receive a complementary Eclipse Foundation Contributing Membership.

Silver Member

Silver members contribute to the development of OpenHW Group’s global ecosystem of open-source, industrial-quality processor IP, and can participate in and contribute to all technical activities and task groups. Silver Member benefits:

  • Entitled to cast one vote on any matter presented to the Members (including the election of directors).
  • Employees are entitled to join and make technical contributions to all OpenHW Group technical committees and projects.
  • Entitled through their OpenHW Group and Eclipse Foundation memberships to join the OpenHW Asia and OpenHW Europe Working Groups.
  • Receive a complementary Eclipse Foundation Contributing Membership.

Supporter Member

Supporter members show their support for OpenHW Group's global ecosystem of open-source, industrial-quality processor IP through their active participation in all matters other than technical contribution. Supporter Member benefits:

  • Entitled to cast one vote on any matter presented to the Members (including the election of directors).
  • Employees are entitled to join all OpenHW Group technical committees and projects.
  • Entitled to join the OpenHW Asia and OpenHW Europe Working Groups subject to also becoming Eclipse Foundation Contributing Members.

How to Become a Member

To become a member, follow these steps below:

Once you have chosen the level of membership (Platinum, Gold, Silver, or Supporter):

  • Complete the Membership Information Form (form #1).
  • Read the OpenHW Bylaws.
  • Complete and sign the Membership Agreement (form #2).
  • For Platinum, Gold and Silver level memberships, complete and sign the Member Committer and Contributor Agreement (form #3). Form #3 is not required for Supporter level memberships.
  • Email us forms #1, #2, and #3 (if applicable), as well as a full colour, vector-based member logo on a transparent background for our OpenHW member directory.
